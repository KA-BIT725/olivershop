# Images Folder

## Required Images for Your Website

Place your product images in this folder with the following names:

### 1. Hero Section
- **hero-baby.jpg** - Main banner image featuring a baby/child in your clothing
  - Recommended size: 800x600px or larger
  - Should be high quality and eye-catching

### 2. Category Images
- **tops.jpg** - T-shirt or top product
- **bottoms.jpg** - Shorts or pants product
- **dresses.jpg** - Dress product
- **new-arrivals.jpg** - Latest product or romper
  - Recommended size: 500x500px each
  - Use clean, centered product photos

### 3. Featured Products
- **product-1.jpg** - First featured product
- **product-2.jpg** - Second featured product
- **product-3.jpg** - Third featured product
- **product-4.jpg** - Fourth featured product
  - Recommended size: 600x600px each
  - High-quality product photography

## Image Guidelines

### Quality
- Use high-resolution images (at least 1000px width)
- Good lighting and clear focus
- Professional product photography preferred

### Background
- White or light neutral backgrounds work best
- Consistent background across all products
- Remove distracting elements

### Format
- Save as JPG for photos (smaller file size)
- Use PNG if you need transparency
- Optimize images before uploading (use TinyPNG.com)

### File Naming
- Use lowercase letters
- Use hyphens instead of spaces
- Keep names descriptive: `blue-tshirt-kids.jpg`

## Temporary Solution

If you don't have images yet, you can:
1. Use placeholder image services:
   - https://placeholder.com/
   - https://via.placeholder.com/600x600
   
2. Example: Add this to your HTML temporarily:
   ```html
   <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/600x600/e8dcc8/1e3a5f?text=Product+Image" alt="Product">
   ```

3. Take photos with your smartphone:
   - Use natural daylight
   - Place product on white paper/cloth
   - Keep camera steady
   - Take multiple angles

## Pro Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Compress images to reduce loading time
- Use consistent aspect ratios
- Show products from multiple angles
- Include size reference if helpful

❌ **Don't:**
- Use watermarked stock photos
- Mix different photography styles
- Upload extremely large files (>2MB each)
- Use blurry or low-quality images

---

**Need help?** Consider hiring a product photographer or using a photo editing service like Canva or Photoshop to prepare your images professionally.
